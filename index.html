About Me

As an Army veteran where I learned attention-to-detail skills, I also learned discipline and developed a strong work ethic. So training to become a coder/software developer was a no brainer because alot of the same principles from the military applies I enjoy learning new things and I have always been interested in coding and computers but didn't believe I had the aptitude for it. However, Eleven Fifty Academy changed my view and I decided to go for it as I was looking to change my career.
